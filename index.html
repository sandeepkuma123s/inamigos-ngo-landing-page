Who We Are

A Movement Born from Compassion

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation was built on a simple belief — that collective action can address society's deepest challenges. Headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, our work spans underserved communities across India, providing food, education, skills, and compassion to those who need it most.


We address root causes of inequality — not just symptoms — through six tightly focused flagship programs. Every rupee donated goes toward building a more inclusive, compassionate, and empowered India.

Our Flagship Initiatives

Six Projects, One Mission

Each project targets a specific gap in society — together, they form a complete ecosystem of care and empowerment.

Project Seva

Serving Humanity with Compassion

Distributing food and clothing to underprivileged families and homeless individuals across communities so no one sleeps hungry.

Project Bachpanshala

Nurturing Young Minds

Ensuring quality education reaches underprivileged children, giving them the foundation to build bright, self-reliant futures.

Project Jeev

Empowering Lives, All Species

Rescuing, feeding, and protecting stray and wild animals — because compassion doesn't stop at human boundaries.

Project Udaan

Soaring Towards Independence

Empowering women through vocational skill development and financial literacy programs that create lasting economic independence.

Project Prakriti

Plant for a Better Tomorrow

Tree plantation drives, environmental awareness campaigns, and sustainability initiatives to protect our natural heritage.

Project Vikas

Building India's Future Workforce

A massive internship and skill-development program that has trained over 30,000 young Indians in digital and professional skills.

Why It Matters

The Change We're Creating

India's underserved communities face intersecting crises. InAmigos works at every intersection.

🍽️

Zero Hunger

Millions still go to bed hungry. Our feeding drives reach families, street children, and the elderly with regular meals.

📚

Education Equity

Poverty breaks the education pipeline early. Bachpanshala bridges the gap for children who would otherwise be left behind.

👩‍💼

Women's Rights

Economic independence is the foundation of gender equality. Udaan gives women the tools to lead their own lives.

🐾

Animal Protection

Stray animals are among the most vulnerable. Jeev ensures they receive care, medical help, and compassion.

🌱

Climate Action

Deforestation threatens livelihoods and biodiversity. Prakriti mobilises communities to plant, protect, and preserve.

🚀

Youth Empowerment

Vikas creates India's next generation of skilled professionals through structured, hands-on internship programs.
